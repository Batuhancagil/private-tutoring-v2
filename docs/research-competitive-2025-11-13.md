## Executive Summary

This competitive intelligence report analyzes key competitors in the private tutoring platform market, with specific focus on **daily progress tracking**, **timeline/calendar assignment systems**, and **student question logging** features. The analysis covers both Turkish market (Edumy) and global competitors (Tutorbase, TutorCruncher, ClassDojo).

### Key Findings

**Market Opportunity:**
- Global private tutoring market: $123.8B (2024), projected $201.8B by 2026 [Verified - 2+ sources]
- CAGR: 8.4% [Verified - 2+ sources]
- Market is fragmented with top 5 companies holding only 30% market share [Verified - 2+ sources]

**Critical Gap Identified:**
Most platforms focus on **session scheduling** and **marketplace matching**. **Few platforms emphasize daily homework/question tracking with timeline-based assignment systems** - this represents a significant differentiation opportunity.

**Competitive Positioning:**
- **Edumy** (Turkey): Closest competitor with weekly planning and assignment allocation, but lacks granular daily question logging
- **Tutorbase**: Strong analytics but focuses on attendance/session tracking, not daily question progress
- **TutorCruncher**: Comprehensive tutor management but limited student-side daily tracking
- **ClassDojo**: Excellent parent-teacher communication but minimal assignment/question tracking

**Recommendation:** Your platform's focus on **daily question logging (right/wrong/empty)** with **timeline-based assignments** fills a clear market gap and provides strong differentiation.
